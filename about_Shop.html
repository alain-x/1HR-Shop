About 1HR Shop

1HR Shop is a premier online retailer offering rugby-inspired fashion and accessories. Our mission is to provide high-quality products that celebrate the spirit of rugby and sportsmanship.

Founded in Rwanda, we take pride in offering the best for rugby fans everywhere, with a focus on comfort, style, and durability.
